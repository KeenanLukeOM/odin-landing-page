Some random information about Balatro.

Add Jokers to your poker deck to gain unique modifiers -- stack multipliers, chips, and money. Not so useless now!

"Level up" the poker hands you already know and love to gain additional score in the game.

Just hit a crazy combo? Balatro counts your score live and plays dramatic effects based on how effective your hand was.
